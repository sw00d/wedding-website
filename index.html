Travel Details

Toll Roads

Once in Mexico, you'll take the toll roads and enjoy stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. The cost is approximately $8–$10 round trip, so make sure you have cash.

Mexican Auto Insurance

Please purchase Mexican auto insurance if you drive your vehicle across the border — U.S. insurance typically does not provide coverage in Mexico.

The Scenic Drive

The coastal highway from Tijuana to Rosarito is a beautiful stretch of Pacific coastline. Enjoy the views — the drive is part of the experience.
